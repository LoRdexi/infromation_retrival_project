### An Evolution of TF-IDF: BM25 (Best Matching 25)

**BM25** is an advanced ranking function used by modern search engines like Elasticsearch. It improves upon the classic TF-IDF model in several smart ways:

- **It solves the limitations of TF-IDF** by normalizing for document length. This prevents longer documents from having an unfair advantage just because they contain more words.
    
- **It handles term frequency more intelligently** with a "saturation factor". This means that after a word appears a certain number of times, its contribution to the relevance score stops growing as quickly.
    
- **It doesn't require the query to be represented as a vector**.
